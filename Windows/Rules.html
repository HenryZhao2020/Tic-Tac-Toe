The game is played on a 3x3 grid.

You are X.

The AI or the other player is O.

Players take turns placing their icons in empty squares.

The first player to get 3 icons in a row (up, down, across, or diagonally) is the winner.

When all 9 squares are full, the game is over.

If no player has 3 icons in a row, the game ends in a tie.
